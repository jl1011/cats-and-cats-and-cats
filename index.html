What kind of cats exist?

Known as the tabby, and colloqually as the r/StandardIssueCat, this cat is what many people think of when they think of cats. They're identifiable by their brown coat and the M on their forehead.

This mysterious, playful kitty is known as a Russian Blue. They're known for their beautiful gray-blue coat and beautiful eyes.

Although at first they might look strange, these kitties, known as hairless cats, are just as cute as their furred counterparts. Because they don't have fur, they require regular baths. However, they also have to wear clothing to keep themselves warm - which means adorable pictures like this!

Orange cats are a variant on the tabby - they often have beautiful blue or green eyes, and a vibrant orange coat. They're also rather... silly, so their antics are often documented online, for example on the Reddit subreddit r/OneOrangeBrainCell.
